What are the signs?

The sign language recorded is the American Sign Language. American Sign Language is a natural language that serves as the predominant sign language of Deaf communities in the United States of America and most of Anglophone Canada.

Learning ASL can be a rewarding experience, opening doors to communication and understanding within the Deaf community and offering insights into a vibrant and unique culture.
