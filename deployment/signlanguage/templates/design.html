Glove Design

The glove is embedded with flex sensors, gyroscope and connected to and ESP32 microcontroller.

1. Flex sensors: Flex sensors are utilized to detect and measure the degree of bending or flexing of each finger.

2. Gyroscope: The gyroscope assists in tracking hand movements and orientations, aiding in accurate interpretation and translation of gestures.

3. ESP32 Microcontroller: The ESP32 is a powerful microcontroller that's widely used for various IoT applications, known for its dual-core processing, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth capabilities, and versatile functionality.
